Delivery

Your leaflet will nearly always require an imprint. It should be very similar to this: "Promoted by Michael Coffey for [candidate name or 'Bath and NE Somerset Green Party'], c/o Ravenscroft, Sydney Road, Bath, BA2 6NT. Printed by Solopress, 9 Stock Road, SS2 5QF."

All leaflets will be printed on FSC-certified stock with carbon offsetting (carbon-neutral). Consider stating this on your literature if space allows, eg in small next near/with the imprint.
