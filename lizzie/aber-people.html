I make no apologies for the bad quality of some of these photos- I appear in plenty of bad photos on the web myself and the last dregs of sympathy have been drained away...

I also have no idea in what order some of these events occurred, so I've made a good old guess.

They start off in the summer of 2000, with Hels and Nanooe doing ballet on the seafront at about 3 in the morning (a good place to start, I felt). There was also Mary's hen night, which ended up in the Bay.

After that there is a big jump to the winter of 2000/2001 when I was living in Waunfawr, and at New Year there was snow... with Shaddy being childish!

A bit later in the year, and it was warm enough for a barbeque at Jim's, with Hazel and others. Jim is getting the hose to spray the flying ants, in case you were wondering.

Most of the photos I have are from Claire's leaving do in the late summer, at Agra. We started off in Scholars where I got a picture of Ed and Hels. In Agra we took over most of the restaurant! Arjan, Hilary, Jean and Sharon, Jim and Rich, John and Alison, Nicky and Hywel and Paul amongst others. Anja and designs on the after dinner mints...

The rest of the pictures are from my house in Llanilar. One of Rach before she left at the end of September 2001. Then Shan and Mike moved in... Christmas time visits: Faye, Nick, Greg and Shan.
