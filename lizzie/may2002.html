May 2002 Photos

A few photos from a rare visit in May- peak of Bluebell time... and I was a chalet guest too! What luxury... pity about the gales. These photos, like the ones of my family, are somewhat pixilated, I'll try and sot it out when I have more time.

1.  Sunset taken from near the farm, looking north.

2. Plantago coronopus flowering on the windowsill of the farm near the common room. I got a wee bit over-excited about this, it isn't rare but the were *such* nice specimens... :-)

3. Sea campion near Skomer Head

4. The farm from North Valley, in the bluebells.

5. Kittiwakes flying from the Wick

6. Predictable... a puffin at the Wick. They weren't very obliging this year but I've accumulated millions of photos like this so it is probably just as well!

7. Bracken... looking quite sweet

8. Lichen growing on the farm wall

9. Finally- a bluebell!
