Family Gallery

Here are some photos of my family. I've been far too slow getting these up. And I'll tell you what, one day I'll sit down with more time and get this scanning images thing sussed. I've had to do these in a hurry and these really need re-scanning as they are a bit pixilated but I guess that will have to do for now.

There are pictures from our visit this Easter (2002) to Pembrokeshire... Michael outside the Lobster Pot in Marloes,  my Dad near the Sloop Inn in Porthgain, and all three on the coast path... although I can't quite remember where I took this. 

The rest of the photos I have are from our trip to Skomer in May 2002. The weather was not too great but all the bluebells were out. I managed to get a few photos while the sun was out! My mum in the bluebells, and my Dad sitting on the rocks by the Garland Stone. After I took these it kinda rained non-stop till the last evening before we got rushed off early in the morning due to dire weather warnings- the island boat had already been carried away by the storms! Anyhow these evening shots are  my Dad, and... I didn't get away with it... my mum got one of me too!
